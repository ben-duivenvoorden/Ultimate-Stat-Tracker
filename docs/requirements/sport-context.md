# Sport Context — Ultimate Frisbee
## Ultimate Stat Tracker

**Version:** 0.1
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-17
**Source:** WFDF Rules of Ultimate 2025-2028 (Basic Version)

---

## Fundamentals

- 7-a-side team sport played with a flying disc
- Rectangular field with an end zone at each end
- A team scores by catching the disc in the opponent's end zone
- A thrower cannot run with the disc — must pass
- No referees — self-officiated (Spirit of the Game)

---

## Structure of a Game

- A game ends when one team reaches the score cap (not enforced by this app)
- Half time occurs at a defined mid-point score (not enforced by this app)
- Each game is made up of **points**
- Each point ends with a goal being scored

---

## Structure of a Point

1. Both teams select their line (5–7 players each)
2. The **pulling team** throws the disc to the **receiving team** to start the point
3. The receiving team advances the disc by passing
4. If a pass is incomplete (drop, interception, out of bounds, stall) — **turnover**: possession and attacking direction swap
5. The point ends when a player catches the disc in the end zone they are attacking — **goal**

---

## Direction & Pulling Rules

- The team that **scored** becomes the **pulling team** for the next point
- Teams **switch attacking direction** after every point
- At half time, ends switch once more
- The pulling team starts each point defending; the receiving team attacks

---

## Key Event Types (relevant to stat keeping)

| Event | Description |
|---|---|
| **Pull** | Opening throw to start a point |
| **Pass** | Thrower releases the disc to a teammate |
| **Catch** | Receiver successfully catches a pass |
| **Goal** | Catch in the attacking end zone — point scored |
| **Drop** | Receiver fails to catch a pass — turnover |
| **Interception** (D) | Defender catches or deflects a pass — turnover |
| **Out of Bounds** | Disc lands or is caught out of bounds — turnover |
| **Stall** | Thrower does not release within the stall count — turnover |
| **Foul** | Contact call — can result in disc returning to thrower or turnover depending on outcome |

---

## Stat-Keeping Implications

- Every pass has a **thrower** and an intended **receiver**
- Every catch belongs to a **receiver**
- Every turnover is attributed to a player (dropper, intercepted thrower, stalled thrower)
- Every interception / block (D) is attributed to the **defender**
- Every goal has a **scorer** and an **assist** (the last completed pass)
- Players on the field for each point must be tracked for playing time stats

---

## Notes for This App

- The app does not enforce score caps or half time — it records what happens
- The app does not adjudicate fouls — it records that a foul was called and the outcome
- Brick mark / pull OOB handling is **out of scope**
